Reliable, fast, climate controlled shipping.

How much is shipping?

At Proteaceae, we strive to make shipping both efficient and affordable. Our standard shipping rate starts at $16.95, effective from July 4, 2023. This rate covers the shipment of one to 16 plants, optimized for our packaging that comfortably fits 16 plants. For orders exceeding $320, we are pleased to offer shipping free of charge. For orders that fall between these two values, shipping costs are calculated based on the weight and number of items, using Australia Post Standard Parcel delivery rates.

Before finalizing your purchase, please visit "View And Edit Cart" and enter your postcode to receive an estimate of the shipping costs for your specific order. Addresses in NT, TAS, and WA will incur an additional $10 fee to cover mandatory biosecurity inspections, which is included in the shipping cost. Our address verification software will automatically apply this fee based on your postcode.

Where do you send plants?

Proteaceae is proud to offer nationwide shipping across all states, territories, and Australian islands. We deliver to both street addresses and PO Boxes. Whether you're in a coastal area, hinterland, rainforest, snowfields, or the outback, we've got you covered – from remote locations to bustling city apartments.

When will my plants be packed and shipped?

We prioritize efficiency in our shipping process, packing and shipping orders every weekday, in the order they are received. While we can't specify future packing dates, rest assured that we use Australia Post for delivery whenever possible, or reliable couriers for other orders.

How long will my plants take to arrive?

Once your order is dispatched, you will receive a notification email with a tracking number to monitor your order’s progress. On average, please allow up to 7 days for delivery. During peak gardening seasons or public holidays, this may extend to 10 working days or more. Delivery times are subject to the schedules of AusPost or our courier partners.

For shipments to NT, TAS, and WA, we schedule weekly dispatches to minimize transit time. Please allow up to 14 days from the shipping notification for these deliveries. For the latest information on shipping schedules, please visit the AusPost website.

Will my plants be okay during transit?

Absolutely. At Proteaceae, we pre-treat and specially hydrate all plants before shipping to ensure their vitality, even during unexpected delays. Our plants are prepared to survive up to three weeks in transit. If you encounter any issues upon unpacking, please refer to our 'Refunds & Problems' page for guidance.

Can I change my delivery date or hold my order?

Once orders are packed and handed over to the courier or AusPost, the delivery schedule is out of our control. If you need your plants delivered later, we suggest ordering closer to your desired date. For orders held at our facility, a $15 maintenance fee applies. Please have your order number and payment details ready when you call to arrange this.

Can I pick up from the research facility instead?

Yes, you can. Place your order online and select 'Click & Collect from our research facility' at checkout. We're located just outside Big W Bankstown, NSW, with a collection point at Reception. Please ensure your order is ready and our offices are open before traveling. We're open for pre-booked Click & Collect pick-ups Monday to Thursday, 8.30am to 3.30pm, and Friday, 8.30am to 12 noon.
