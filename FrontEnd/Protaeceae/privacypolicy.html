Introduction

At Proteaceae, we are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our customers' personal information. This policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and sharing of personal information.

Information Collection

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Contact details (such as name, address, email, and phone number)
  • Payment and transaction information
  • Information about your interests in our products and services
  • Any other information you choose to provide to us

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect may be used for the following purposes:

  • To process and fulfill your orders
  • To communicate with you about our products and services
  • To improve our products, services, and website
  • For market research and analysis
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations

Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or lease your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information with:

  • Service providers who assist us in our operations (such as delivery and payment services)
  • When required by law or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Proteaceae, our customers, or others

Security of Your Information

We take reasonable steps to ensure that your personal information is protected from unauthorized access, misuse, loss, or alteration.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information held by us. Please contact us if you wish to exercise these rights.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will always be posted on our website.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this policy or our privacy practices, please contact us.
